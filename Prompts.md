## Free movie databases like tmdb
If you’re looking for **free movie databases like TMDb**, you’ve got quite a few solid options depending on whether you want **API access, tracking, or just browsing movies like a cinephile detective 🕵️‍♀️🎬**.

---

## 🔥 Best Free Alternatives to TMDb

### 1. Open Movie Database (OMDb) API

* Great if you're building apps/projects
* Provides movie details like ratings, cast, plot, posters
* Simple and lightweight (but smaller database than TMDb) ([Nutritionjobs][1])

👉 Best for: **Developers / projects**

---

### 2. Simkl

* Tracks movies, TV shows, anime
* Syncs watch history across devices
* Has recommendations + watchlists ([AlternativeTo][2])

👉 Best for: **Tracking what you watch**

---

### 3. Trakt.tv

* Automatically tracks what you watch
* Strong API + integrations (Kodi, Plex etc.)
* Social + discovery features ([AlternativeTo][2])

👉 Best for: **Automation + ecosystem**

---

### 4. Letterboxd

* Social platform for movie lovers
* Rate, review, create lists
* Huge community vibe 🍿

👉 Best for: **Reviews + community**

---

### 5. JustWatch

* Shows **where to watch movies legally**
* Filters by OTT platforms (Netflix, Prime, etc.)
* Not focused on reviews, more on availability ([Theme Circle][3])

👉 Best for: **Finding where to stream**

---

### 6. AllMovie

* Clean, simple, no clutter
* Focuses only on movies (no TV shows)
* Old-school database vibes ([MakeUseOf][4])

👉 Best for: **Basic movie info browsing**

---
